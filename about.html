OLIVE INTERIOR PROFILE

Welcome to the grand refreshing gateway to a new world of interior designs exclusively created by Sayali Belsare- Interior Designer based in Pune-the Oxford of the East. She has crafted interior solutions for a variety of clients in retail like Restaurants, Ice cream parlors, Hotels with commercial spaces like Offices, sample flats, factory with factory outlets and many individual homes. Born in Dapoli; the rich cultural konkan belt of costal Maharashtra; Sayali is a inborn designer. She develops a unique design with beautiful yet simplistic layouts, well balanced arrangements nicely blended with optimum space utilization. The interior solutions flow in a smooth and soothing rhythm giving a rich blend of class and luxury. Sayali has developed a skill of understanding the needs, identifying the best requirements to the purpose and combining a sublime ideation to magnify the client’s purpose.

Sayali has an eye for every minute detail be it selecting colors, lighting, materials and furniture to set up the right ambience for the client's desired purpose. She has the aptitude for providing various design combinations that enrich the property with beauty, space, convenience and most important- feeling of satisfaction. Sayali has the unique ability to put in life through her design, layout and furniture arrangements that combine in a golden mean of aesthetics, ergonomics and comfort. Her inquisitive nature keeps her busy to explore new ideas in architecture and interior through various field trips, research programs and designers forum. Sayali’s approach of modern developmental strategies help her craft new and elegant solutions to every client meticulously customizing with the client requirements and purpose.Olive Interiors specializes in a unique blend of ergonomics (optimum space utilization) science of element planning in a planned proportion and logical aesthetics. Every aspect of the property is checked for its physical structure, expected purpose and customer requirements. We believe that the property you dwell or work in; is alive. It breathes energy fuelling our emotions, moods and passions. When the interior designing is rightly managed positivity and prosperity prevails.
