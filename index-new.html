OLIVE INTERIOR PROFILE

Welcome to the grand refreshing gateway to a new world of interior designs exclusively created by Sayali Belsare- Interior Designer based in Pune-the Oxford of the East. She has crafted interior solutions for a variety of clients in retail like Restaurants, Ice cream parlors, Hotels with commercial spaces like Offices, sample flats, factory with factory outlets and many individual homes. Born in Dapoli; the rich cultural konkan belt of costal Maharashtra; Sayali is a inborn designer. She develops a unique design with beautiful yet simplistic layouts, well balanced arrangements nicely blended with optimum space utilization.
